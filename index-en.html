SUBSCRIPTION 
To have a family doctor in the PrivaMED network, you must become a member by signing in to a subscription. You only pay for one annual subscription to gain access to the entire network. The subscription gives each member of the subscription personal access to Internet services. Access to the electronic health record is individual for people 14 years of age and over.

Types of subscription**:

  • Solo: $145 (one person)
  • Duo: $265 (two people)
  • Family*: $325 (three to six people)

*Based on a maximum of two people aged 21 and over and four people under age of 21.

** Payment for a subscription can be made on an annual or monthly basis. Subscriptions are always for a period of one year, renewable by tacit agreement. GST and QST are added to the subscription prices.

INFILTRATIONS

What is the difference between an ultrasound-guided injection and a non-ultrasound injection?

Ultrasound-guided injection : 

Ultrasound is used to guide the injection of the medication into a targeted area. This allows the doctor to visualize the needle and the area to be treated in real-time.

  • Cortisone injection under ultrasound (1 site) ; $200
  • Cortisone injection under ultrasound (2 sites) ; $300

Non-ultrasound injection :

The medication is injected blindly, without the aid of ultrasound to guide the needle. The doctor typically relies on the patient's anatomy and their experience to locate the injection site.

  • Cortisone injection (per site) ; $100

*Please note that consultation fees and viscosupplementation (if desired) are not included*
